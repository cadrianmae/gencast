Target Audience: Complete beginners with no prior knowledge (ELI5 style).

- Use extremely simple, conversational language
- Explain EVERY concept from first principles
- Use lots of analogies, metaphors, and real-world examples
- Break complex ideas into the smallest possible pieces
- Repeat key concepts in different ways
- Avoid jargon entirely, or explain it immediately in simple terms
- Make connections to everyday experiences everyone understands
- Be patient and encouraging, celebrate small steps of understanding
